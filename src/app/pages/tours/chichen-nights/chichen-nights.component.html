Summary

If it´s amazing at day imagine it at night!
We will pick you up at your hotel and take you to Valladolid, Yucatan where you will visit a Temazcal and partake in an ancient mayan ritual of purification.
After that you will refresh in the sacred fresh waters of a Cenote in the center of Valladolid city in which you will have time to explore the beauties of the colonial city in which the mayan traditions are still honored.

The archeological site of Chichen-Itza was named world heritage by UNESCO in 1988. It is one of the most famous tourist points in the continent and home of the Kukulkan´s temple named one of the new seven wonders of the world in which you will see the incredible light and sound show “Noches de Kukulkan”.

What to expect
