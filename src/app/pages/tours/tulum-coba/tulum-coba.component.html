Summary

The archaeological sites of Tulum and Coba are amongst the most important and representative sites in the area. Coba contains one of the biggest temples in the peninsula and whose climb is still allowed today. Climb to the top of the huge Mayan temple and contemplate the Mayan jungle as far as the eyes can see. Discover the walled city of Tulum and be dazzled by the turquoise waters of the Caribbean Sea that hits its shores.

Choosing us guarantees you the best possible experience and the customization you deserve. Let us know how we can make your experience the most special way possible for you and your companions and we will be happy to assist you.

Your private tours includes your private tour guide, and the snacks and beverages of your choice in the transport unit, snorkel and swimming in one of the best beaches in the world or Cenote.

What to expect

Although we suggest a certain time for pick up in order to maximize the time available and be able to travel among the wonders avoiding the crowds, the time for pick up is yours to decide. We will pick you up at your hotel in a private van with air conditioning with your guide and amenities of your choice. We will travel first to Coba where optionally we will travel the archaeological site in bicycle, tricycle or by foot. Your guide will show you the most important structures and you will have the opportunity to climb the temple of Nohoch Mul, one of the biggest Mayan structures in the area.

Once in Tulum, you will get to know the most important edification in the archaeological site y you will free time to walk and marvel at the landscapes crowned by one of the best beaches in Riviera Maya. Refresh in the cold waters of a Cenote or enjoy the sand and the sea in one of the best beaches in the country.

This is your tour! Plan with us to receive you with the beverages, snacks and music of your choice! Avoid tight schedules in crowded buses, you decide your schedules and which sites to visit! During the duration of your tour we will be happy to suggest points of interest in the area.
